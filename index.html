The model scales
Phase 1
Outreach
We reach your highest-risk members first — those without an AWV in 12+ months or flagged for SDOH needs. Up to six outreach attempts across five channels per member.
Phase 2 · Months 2–4
Engage
When members connect, we enroll them, complete SDOH screenings, match them with a dedicated care team member, and begin removing barriers to care. This is where activation starts.
What you're likely wondering right now "Are members actually responding to this?" Members are actively engaging with the program. A majority of enrolled members are progressing beyond initial outreach into needs-based conversations — identifying barriers like food access, transportation, and care coordination. 16.7% enrollment conversion in Month 1 says yes, they're responding.
Enrollment by Market
Phase 3 · Months 4–6
Activate
We move members from engagement to action — facilitating PCP visits, resolving SDOH needs, connecting to benefits, and supporting reward redemption and AWV attestation.
What you'll be wondering at Month 4 "Is engagement leading to action?" Engagement is translating into member-reported care actions. Enrolled members are more likely to attest to scheduling or completing recommended care activities. These trends indicate early behavior changes consistent with improved likelihood of closing care gaps. Activation metrics populate here starting Month 4.
Data note: Activation outcomes (PCP connections, SDOH resolutions, reward redemptions, AWV attestations) populate monthly from Pyx Tableau dashboards. These metrics feed directly into the value story in Phase 4.
Phase 4 · Months 6–9
Impact & Value
The "so what." Sustained engagement and member-reported actions support movement toward quality and care engagement goals. Engagement trends suggest increasing likelihood of members completing recommended care activities within the measurement period — directly tied to CAHPS and Stars performance.
What you'll be wondering at Month 6 "Is this helping us improve performance?" Value estimates below are research-backed projections based on enrolled members today. Actual outcomes replace projections as Tableau data flows monthly. Star rating movement — the ultimate performance indicator — is a 12–18 month lagging measure. The leading indicators (screenings, PCP connections, member experience) appear first.
